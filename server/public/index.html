EMAIL QUALITY

Bankers can spot an AI email in two seconds.

Most AI tools produce the same generic, filler-heavy templates. Finance professionals see through them immediately. ColdMatch is trained to write emails that sound like you wrote them, specific, concise, and focused on a real connection.

AI Slop
To: ryan.chen@gs.com
Subject: Aspiring Finance Professional Seeking Guidance
Hi Ryan, I hope this email finds you well! My name is Justin Rodriguez and I am a sophomore at the University of Michigan Ross School of Business. I came across your LinkedIn profile and was truly impressed by your remarkable journey from Michigan Ross to Goldman Sachs — a path that really resonated with me and aligns perfectly with my career goals as an aspiring finance professional. I am deeply passionate about breaking into investment banking — specifically the kind of high-impact advisory work that Goldman is known for — and would greatly appreciate any insights you could share about your path and the recruiting process. A quick call at your earliest convenience would mean the world to me as I look to gain invaluable guidance from someone who has navigated this journey so successfully. I have attached my resume below for your reference. Warm regards, Justin Rodriguez
ColdMatch
To: ryan.chen@gs.com
Subject: Michigan Ross Student Interested in IB at Goldman
Hi Ryan, I hope you are doing well. My name is Justin Rodriguez and I am a sophomore studying Business at Michigan Ross interested in IB opportunities at Goldman. I noticed you also went to Ross before transitioning to M&A at Goldman and I was wondering if you would be available for a quick call in the coming weeks. I would be more than happy to find a time that works for you. In case it is helpful, I have attached my resume for reference. Looking forward to hearing from you. Best, and Go Blue! Justin

No filler words. No em dashes. No generic openers. Just a short, specific, human email.
